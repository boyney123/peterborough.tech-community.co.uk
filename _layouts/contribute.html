What could you contribute?

If you want to be apart of a learning, sharing and growing community now is your chance to get involved.

This open source project is the perfect platform for any level of blogger.

We are looking for blog posts on anything IT related. Here are just a few ideas to get you thinking:

Agile Practices
Business

Local Events

Local promotion
Ideas

Why and how can you contribute?

We believe you can learn something new from everybody we meet. So what knowledge, story, idea, or discussion could you share?

You can contribute within minutes and contributing is simple.

You can either use Github or send an email with your post and details.
